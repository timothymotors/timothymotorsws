About Us

Welcome to Timothy Motors, your trusted destination for high-quality, secondhand vehicles. Located in Tochigi, Japan, we pride ourselves on offering an extensive range of four-wheelers and two-wheelers that fit every budget and lifestyle.

At Timothy Motors, our mission is to provide our customers with reliable, affordable vehicles and exceptional customer service. Every vehicle in our showroom undergoes a thorough inspection to ensure it meets our high standards for safety and performance. We believe in transparency and integrity, which is why each car comes with a detailed history report and warranty options.

Whether you're a first-time buyer or looking to upgrade your current vehicle, our friendly and knowledgeable team is here to help you find the perfect match. We offer flexible payment options tailored to your needs, making the buying process smooth and stress-free.

Visit us today or explore our online channels. Drive away with confidence, knowing you've made a smart and secure investment.
